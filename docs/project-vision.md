# Project Vision

`nemesis-trainer` is a web tool where users paste or build a team and receive an adversarial trainer designed to beat it.

The product should answer:

- What is my team weak to?
- What kind of trainer punishes those weaknesses?
- What would that trainer lead?
- What is their first plan?
- What can I change to stop losing to that matchup?

## Core Loop

1. Paste or build a Pokemon Showdown-style team.
2. Run a weakness audit.
3. Generate one or more boss trainers that punish the team's gaps.
4. Explain why each matchup is difficult.
5. Suggest counterplay and small team edits.
6. Share the generated challenge with a URL.

## Differentiator

Do not frame this as a generic AI team builder.

Generic team builders say:

> Here is a good team.

Nemesis Trainer says:

> Here is the team that punishes yours.

That adversarial framing is more distinctive, more shareable, and better suited to repeat use.

## Product Principles

- Useful before it is clever.
- Explainable before it is magical.
- Static and cheap before it is backend-heavy.
- Shareable before it requires accounts.
- Format-focused before it attempts full franchise coverage.

## Possible Public Positioning

Strong names and taglines:

- `Nemesis Trainer`: "The trainer built to beat you."
- `TeamBreaker`: "Paste your team. Meet its worst matchup."
- `BossCheck`: "Find the boss your team cannot beat."
- `Final Gym`: "Every team has a final test."
- `CounterTrainer`: "Generate your team's hard counter."

Avoid public names that include `Pokemon`, `Cynthia`, or `GPT`.
