# AI Arena Seed Teams

These teams seed the internal AI arena and heuristic evolution tools. They are Showdown `gen9ou` Singles imports, because the arena currently validates and simulates teams with Pokemon Showdown's `gen9ou` format.

## Source Policy

I researched public Pokemon Champions team resources on May 13, 2026. The currently discoverable Champions top-team pages are mostly VGC/doubles tournament sheets or game-specific stat-point builds, not directly importable Showdown OU Singles teams. Using those files in this arena would either fail validation or train the evaluator against the wrong format.

Until this project supports a Champions-specific Singles ruleset, seed the training pool from current/recent Smogon SV OU Singles RMTs with public PokePaste imports.
